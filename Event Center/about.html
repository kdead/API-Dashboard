Inside the Velvet Note

An upscale and intimate live-music lounge where good energy and good music come together.

Our Story

Founded in 2026, The Velvet Note has become a destination for music lovers seeking an unforgettable and intimate experience. It was created for people who appreciate soulful music, handcrafted cocktails, delicious bites, and unforgettable vibes. We are committed to providing a welcoming and inclusive space where everyone can enjoy the magic of live music.

Our Mission

Our mission is to create a vibrant and inclusive community where music lovers can come together to experience the magic of live performance. We are dedicated to showcasing a diverse range of musical talent, from local artists to internationally renowned performers, while providing exceptional service and a welcoming atmosphere for all our guests.

What We Offer

  • Live Music: We feature a diverse lineup of live music performances, including jazz, blues, soul, funk, and more. Our stage has hosted both local talent and internationally renowned artists.
  • Handcrafted Cocktails and Curated Wine Selections: Our skilled mixologists create a menu of handcrafted cocktails that perfectly complement the music and atmosphere. From classic concoctions to innovative creations, our drinks are designed to delight your palate.
  • Delicious Bites: Our kitchen serves up a selection of delicious bites that pair perfectly with our cocktails and live music. Whether you're in the mood for something light or a more substantial meal, we have options to satisfy every craving.
  • Unforgettable Vibes: At The Velvet Note, we pride ourselves on creating an unforgettable atmosphere where guests can relax, connect, and enjoy the magic of live music in an intimate setting.
